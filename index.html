--Frequently Asked Question--

What documents do I need to rent a vehicle?

To rent a vehicle, you typically need a valid driver’s license, a credit card in your name, and proof of insurance. Some companies may also require a second form of ID

What is the minimum age to rent a car?

The minimum age to rent a car is usually 21 years old. However, drivers under 25 may incur a young driver surcharge and have limited vehicle options

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

yes, most rental companies allow you to modify or cancel your reservation without penalty up to 24 hours before your pickup time. Check your confirmation for specific terms

What should I do if I get into an accident?

If you’re involved in an accident, ensure everyone is safe, contact local authorities, and notify the rental company immediately. They will guide you through the claims process
